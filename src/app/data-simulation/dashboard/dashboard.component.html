Welcome to disease simulation app

In this application you can simulate spread of disease. There are some initial data to describe disease and group of population.

First we need give number of population and initial number of infected people.

Second we have to give the period of time of simulation, necessary time to recover and time after that infected person can die. All period of time we give in days

Finally we have two factors to describe type of disease:

  1. First one is factor of spread of disease. This factor describe how fast disease spreads. If this factor is equal or less than 1 that disease will not spread

  2. Second one is mortality factor. This factor describe the probability of death ill person. Maximal value of this factor is 1 that means 100% probability of death that means all ill people will die. Minimal value is 0 that means no ill person will die.
